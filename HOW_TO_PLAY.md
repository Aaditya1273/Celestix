## 1. Establishing Your Identity (iNFTs)
Before you enter the village, you have the option to mint an **iNFT (Intelligent NFT)**. 
*   **What is it?** This is your immutable, on-chain identity. It acts as your avatar and profile within the ecosystem, deployed exclusively on the **0G Network**.
*   **Where is it?** You can view your minted identities in the **My Portfolio** tab. Once equipped, this avatar represents you on the Global Leaderboard.

## 2. Choosing Your Path (Settings & Staking)
When you click **Start New Game**, you are presented with a crucial choice via the Game Settings interface.

### A. Game Modes
*   **Casual Mode (Free)**: Play for fun. You simply sign a gas-less `personal_sign` message via MetaMask to authorize the backend to anchor your game state to the **0G Network**. You still earn small base rewards for winning.
*   **Ranked Mode (Stake FOG)**: Put your money where your mouth is. You approve and stake 10, 50, or 100 **FOG Tokens** into the **0G StakingManager smart contract**. 
    *   *If you win*, your stake is refunded and your final reward payout is multiplied by **2.0x**.
    *   *If you lose*, your staked tokens are permanently **confiscated** into the protocol treasury.

### B. Difficulty Levels
*   **Easy**: NPCs are friendly and eager to help. (1.0x Reward Multiplier)
*   **Normal**: NPCs are cautious; you must build familiarity before they reveal clues. (1.5x Reward Multiplier)
*   **Hard**: NPCs are deeply paranoid. One wrong dialogue choice can lock you out of crucial clues permanently. (2.0x Reward Multiplier)

*Note: Multipliers stack. Playing Hard mode while Staked yields massive FOG returns.*

## 3. The Core Gameplay Loop
You awaken in a fog-drenched village with no memory of how you got there. Your objective is to find your lost friends by deducing the **"Correct Location"** where they are trapped.

1.  **Interact**: Speak with the AI villagers. Type your own custom prompts.
2.  **Build Trust**: Every conversation is processed by the AI Engine. If you are rude, the NPC's `familiarity` with you drops, and they will refuse to speak. If you are kind and logical, they will reveal vital clues.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **0G Storage Integration**  
> **On-Chain Anchoring**: Behind the scenes, the root hash of your evolving AI dialogue tree is continuously uploaded and anchored to the **0G Decentralized Storage Network**. This guarantees that your entire game history and state are immutable, provable, and verifiable.

## 4. The Climax & Resolution
Once you believe you know where your friends are, you click **End Game** and make your final accusation.

*   **Victory**: If you guess the correct location, the backend calculates your base score based on how efficiently you solved the mystery. This score is then multiplied by your Difficulty and Staking multipliers. 
    *   *Profit*: The smart contract refunds your stake (if Ranked) and mints your massive FOG reward directly to your wallet.
*   **Defeat**: If you guess wrong, you succumb to the fog. You receive 0 rewards, and if you played Ranked, your staked FOG is confiscated.

## 5. The Global Leaderboard
Whether you win or lose, your final run is cryptographically verified and posted to the **Global Leaderboard**. 
Here, the community can see your Wallet Address, your equipped iNFT Avatar, your final score, and whether you survived the fog.
